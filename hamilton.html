Sahana,

I really really hope that at some point in your life you see Hamilton live. It's a tragedy, but you may not ever get the chance. I made this just in case this tragedy occurs. The original cast recording is great, but it's not the same as the live show. So, I've compiled this list for you. I found the best version (in my opinion) of each song that I possibly could. Sadly, I couldn not find a decent version of many of the tracks, so I've just replaced those songs with links to the original cast recording, sometimes with an animation. Since you've heard the original cast recording a million times, I include covers more often than perhaps I should; if there's a cover that's almost as good as the original, I usually include it. Sorry this isn't a youtube playlist; I would have made that but I couldn't find the grammy performance of the opening number on youtube and some of these have timestamps... I hope you Enjoy!
I love you,
Aaron
